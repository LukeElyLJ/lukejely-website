About

Hi, I’m Luke.

I’m an audio engineer with a background in recording, mixing, mastering and post-production sound. I studied Recording, Mixing and Production at the University of West London and completed a Master’s in Film and TV with Sound at the University of York.

I work with Pro Tools, Logic Pro, iZotope RX, FabFilter, Waves and spatial audio techniques to create clean, professional and emotionally effective sound.
