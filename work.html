Student Short Film

Rat Trap

Rat Trap was a dialogue-heavy short film where I worked as Sound Supervisor. Production audio was captured using a Sound Devices 888 recorder, Sony UWP wireless systems and a Sennheiser MKH-416.

In post-production I used tools including iZotope RX and Waves Clarity to clean dialogue, reduce unwanted noise and repair difficult production audio. I also built atmosphere, added sound design and used mix automation to support the film’s tone and final presentation.
