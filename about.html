Bio

Hi, I’m Luke.

I’m an audio engineer with experience across recording, mixing, mastering, dialogue editing, sound design and creative audio for film, music and visual media. My work combines technical audio repair and mix skills with a creative approach to tone, atmosphere and storytelling.

I studied Recording, Mixing and Production at the University of West London and completed a Master’s in Film and TV with Sound at the University of York. Across my projects I’ve worked with live instruments, vocals, foley, spatial audio techniques, short films, adverts and music releases.

I use tools including Pro Tools, Logic Pro, iZotope RX, FabFilter, Waves and Melodyne, and I’m especially interested in work that sits between music, film sound and polished creative audio.
