Bio

Hi, I’m Luke.

I’m an audio engineer with experience across recording, mixing, mastering, dialogue editing, sound design and creative audio for film, music and visual media.

I studied Recording, Mixing and Production at the University of West London and completed a Master’s in Film and TV with Sound at the University of York.

My work combines technical audio repair and mix skills with a creative approach to tone, atmosphere and storytelling.
